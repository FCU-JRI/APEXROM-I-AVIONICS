FC Event Log Dictionary

為了大幅節省 LoRa 無線射頻頻寬，系統已將原本 64 Bytes 的純文字事件日誌 (String Log)
壓縮為僅 16 Bytes 的二進位編碼 [uint8_t event_id] + [float p1] + [float p2] + [float p3]。
地面站 (monitor.py) 收到後會自動查表並還原成下列的完整文字。

ID (DEC) 事件名稱 (Macro) 還原文字格式 (Reconstructed String) 參數說明 (Parameters)
1 EVT_STATE_TRANSITION State: {p1} -> {p2} p1=舊狀態, p2=新狀態
2 EVT_SM_CURRENT_STATE [SM] Current State: {p1}, Time: {p2} p1=目前狀態, p2=執行時間(秒)
3 EVT_POWERED_FLIGHT_START EVENT: Powered flight start time recorded -
4 EVT_SYS_RESET ACTION: System Reset to STBY_IDLE -
5 EVT_CALIB_GYRO ACTION: Calibrating Gyro... -
6 EVT_DROGUE_DEPLOY ACTION: Drogue Deployed -
7 EVT_MOTOR_SEP_MAIN ACTION: Motor Sep & Main Chute -
8 EVT_MAIN_NO_SEP ACTION: Main Chute (No Sep) -
9 EVT_MISSION_TERMINATED EVENT: Mission Terminated -
10 EVT_TRIG_FORCE_APOGEE TRIGGER: Global Time Safety - Forcing Apogee (35s) -
11 EVT_TRIG_MOTOR_THRUST TRIGGER: Motor Thrust detected (IMU) -
12 EVT_TRIG_ALT_GAIN TRIGGER: Altitude gain detected (BMP Backup) -
13 EVT_TRIG_BURNOUT_IMU TRIGGER: Burnout detected (IMU) -
14 EVT_TRIG_BURNOUT_TIME TRIGGER: Burnout assumed (Time Backup) -
15 EVT_TRIG_APOGEE_SENSORS TRIGGER: Apogee detected (Sensors) -
16 EVT_TRIG_DESCENT TRIGGER: Descent detected -
17 EVT_DECISION_GPS_FAIL_MAIN DECISION: GPS Failed, forcing Main Chute (Safety First) -
18 EVT_TRIG_LAUNCH_G_FORCE TRIGGER: Launch G-force detected -
19 EVT_GYRO_CALIB_STARTED ACTION: Gyro Calibration Started -
20 EVT_GYRO_CALIB_DONE ACTION: Gyro Calib Done ({p1}, {p2}, {p3}) p1=Bias X, p2=Bias Y, p3=Bias Z
21 EVT_HW_ERROR_IMU HW ERROR: ICM20948 (IMU) Init Failed -
22 EVT_HW_ERROR_BMP HW ERROR: BMP388 (Barometer) Init Failed -
